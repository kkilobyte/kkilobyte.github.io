      parrot.live

      An initiative, using frames from terminal-parrot, to allow any computer with curl to display an animated party parrot.

      Try it!

      curl parrot.live

      :partyparrot:

      Thanks

      • jmhobbs for terminal-parrot, and for the animation frames
      • Robert Koch and Eric Jiang for testing it, and giving feedback ⭐
